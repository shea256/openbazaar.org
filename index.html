OpenBazaar

Zero Fees. Zero Censorship.

OpenBazaar is a new online marketplace
enabling instant, decentralized trade with anyone, anywhere, using bitcoin


Zero fees

OpenBazaar isn't controlled by a central company, or organization. This means there are no mandatory fees to be paid, unlike current online marketplaces.

Privacy

OpenBazaar operates on a decentralized network, meaning your identity and location stays completely anonymous and private.

Cheaper prices

Since OpenBazaar charges no mandatory fees, users generally price items lower due to them not having to make up for seller fees.

Open source

The OpenBazaar codebase is open source, which means anyone has the right to review and make changes to it.
